I will wait

Our distance is like Milky Way and Andromeda,
Even traveling at the speed of light, it will take a million years to reach
It can’t be seen by the naked eye but I feel you are at my side
Daydreaming that we hold each other hand,
And do nothing all day long.

Distance is the biggest burden in life,
It can cost time and effort,
Multiplied by the temptation in the surroundings,
But despite of that, we gonna conquer them all,
Through faith, loyalty and patient, we’re unstoppable.

I might be lost in dark and loneliest road,
Pretending to be normal but mentally challenged,
Seeking the light in the darkest doom,
Even we barely see each other but remember this,
I will wait!
